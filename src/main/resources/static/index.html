Pin2Fix is a geo-tagged issue reporting system that connects citizens with government authorities to quickly resolve community problems.

Key Features

Everything you need to report and track community issues

Geo-Location Based

Pinpoint exact issue locations using GPS. Attach photos as evidence for better clarity.

Real-time Tracking

Track your complaint status from submission to completion with real-time updates.

Multi-level Approval

Complaints go through proper government channels ensuring accountability.

Instant Notifications

Get notified at every stage of your complaint resolution process.

Feedback System

Rate the resolution quality. Negative feedback triggers rework automatically.

Evidence Based

Workers submit completion evidence for verification before final approval.

How It Works

Simple 6-step process from complaint to resolution

1

Citizen Reports Issue

Citizens raise complaints with geo-location and photo evidence through the app.

2

Government Body Review

Government authority reviews the complaint and forwards it to the appropriate department.

3

Department Head Assignment

Department head assigns the task to an area head or directly to field workers.

4

Worker Completes Task

Field worker completes the work and uploads evidence of completion.

5

Multi-level Approval

Head approves the work, then government body gives final confirmation.

6

Citizen Feedback

Citizen is notified and provides feedback. Negative feedback triggers rework.
